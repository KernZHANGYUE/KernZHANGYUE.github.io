Basketball Robot

2023-06-24

Basketball Robot

Research Group of FutureX Robot, School of Instrumentation and Science, Southeast University

Work


⚫ Designed and built a robot system with ROS as the control platform, which could realize founctions
of sensing obstacles, planning paths, identifying different modes, and completing some mechanical
actions
⚫ Achieved the purpose that the robot could accurately identify the position of the basketball by using
the NanoDet ultra-lightweight deep neural networks and the Gamma changes
⚫ Implemented a multi-layer restricted PID control structure to enable the robot to reach the specified
position at the fastest speed
⚫ Proposed a datagram-based communication mechanism for data interoperability between MCU
(STM32) and high-performance processor (TX2)
⚫ Researched on a CNN-based method for object detection and fusion with ROS system
⚫ Obtained a 20% improvement in the robot’s operating stability and ball pickup mechanism
