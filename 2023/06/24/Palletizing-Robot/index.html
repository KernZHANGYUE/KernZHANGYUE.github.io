Palletizing Robot

ERCC China Education Robotics Competition, China Artificial Intelligence Society, Hunan Province
Palletizing Robot Design

Work

⚫ Designed a handling palletizing robot based on infrared patrol and ultrasonic distance detection,
which could recognize color of the blocks and accurately stack them at the designated locations
⚫ Designed a Heuristics-based A* Search handling strategy that ensured the robots could complete
handling tasks at the fastest possible speed
⚫ Designed a motor speed measurement device based on an optical encoder to detect the travel distance
of a robot by using Solidworks
